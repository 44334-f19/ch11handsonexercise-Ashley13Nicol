What are Internet Copyrights?

Internet copyright laws give the original authors or artists the right to exclude others from copying their work or claiming it as their own. While online copyright protection does not protect facts, ideas, systems, or methods of operation, it may protect the way these things are expressed. For more information click Here!

What could happen if you where to break copyright laws?

There are two types of penalties that could be given, civil or criminal charges. If you are found guilty of a civil charge for just misuse of the material you could be charged a fine and legal fees from $200 to $150,000. If you used copyrighted material to make a profit then it is considered a criminal charge and you could be subjected to five years in jail, financial damages and legal costs.
